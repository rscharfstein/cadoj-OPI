Mission

Our mission is to support the California Department of Justice in delivering fair and impartial justice to all Californians. We provide agency staff with the tools and resources to create a world-class justice system.

Values

  • Humility
  • Resilience
  • Integrity
  • Passion
  • Leadership

What we do

  • ENTERPRISE EVALUATION. We uncover enterprise-wide risks and opportunities, conduct and coordinate program assessments, and recommend areas of improvement.
  • PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT. We provide visibility on departmental initiatives and facilitate goalsetting within units and on key projects.
  • CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT. We partner with staff to improve existing services by leveraging cutting edge tools and thinking.
  • INNOVATION DELIVERY. We pilot, evangelize, and launch new products and services to tackle 21st century challenges.
