Mission

Our mission is to empower agency staff with the tools and resources to enforce laws that protect all Californians and deliver fair and impartial justice to all our constituents.

What we do

  • ENTERPRISE EVALUATION. Uncover enterprise-wide risks and opportunities, conduct and coordinate program assessments, and recommend areas of improvement.
  • PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT. Provide visibility on departmental initiatives and facilitate goalsetting within units and on key projects.
  • CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT. Partner with staff to improve existing services by leveraging cutting edge tools and thinking.
  • INNOVATION DELIVERY. Pilot, evangelize, and launch new products and services to tackle 21st century challenges.


Our Approach


We put users first

We work with internal and external users -- agency staff and constituents -- on challenges they face. We see user-centered design as core to our work.

We are data-driven

We believe that data can be a powerful resource to influence change. We leverage data to make informed decisions.

We are digital forward

We're working to bring government into the 21st century. We use technology to improve business processes.

We focus on delivery

We facilitate implementation of cross-departmental projects. We ensure agency staff have the tools and resources to deliver on projects.
